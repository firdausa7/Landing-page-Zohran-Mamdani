About Zohran

Get to know the candidate

Fighting for a More Equitable NYC

Zohran Mamdani is a Democratic Socialist and member of the New York State Assembly representing parts of Queens. Born and raised in New York City, he has dedicated his career to fighting for working-class New Yorkers.

As a state legislator, Zohran has championed progressive policies including universal rent control, Medicare for All, and the Green New Deal. He believes in a city where everyone has access to safe, affordable housing, quality healthcare, and well-funded public services.

Now, Zohran is running for Mayor of New York City to bring his bold, progressive vision to the five boroughs and fight for the many, not the few.

Our Platform

Key priorities for a better NYC

Housing for All

Fight for universal rent control and massive public investment in affordable housing to end the homelessness crisis.

Healthcare Justice

Expand access to quality healthcare for all New Yorkers, regardless of income or immigration status.

Transportation Equity

Invest in reliable, affordable public transportation and make our streets safer for pedestrians and cyclists.

Education for All

Fully fund our public schools, reduce class sizes, and make CUNY tuition-free for NYC residents.

Climate Action

Implement a Green New Deal for NYC to create union jobs while fighting climate change.

Racial Justice

Address systemic racism in policing, housing, education, and economic opportunity.
